🖨️ How to Print:

  1. Print to PDF:
    • Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac)
    • ✅ CHECK "Background graphics"
    • Select "Save as PDF"
    • Choose "Landscape" orientation
    • Set to 11" × 8.5"
    • Save the PDF
  2. Professional Printing:
    • Use the PDF created above
    • Specify: 11" × 8.5" trifold brochure
    • Paper: 100lb gloss or matte cover stock
    • Finish: Scored and folded
